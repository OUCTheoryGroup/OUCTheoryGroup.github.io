由中国海洋大学承办的第11届IEEE国际意识科学与技术会议（iCAST 2020）将于2020年10月26-28在中国青岛举行。欢迎就与人工智能、软计算、智能系统、感知、认知科学、心理学、数据分析、物联网有关的各种主题举行Special Session。

The 11th IEEE International Conference on Awareness Science and Technology (iCAST 2020) will be held in Qingdao, China, October 26-28, 2020. Special sessions on various subjects related to artificial intelligence, softcomputing, intelligent systems, perception, cognitive science, psychology, data analysis, IoT are welcome.
