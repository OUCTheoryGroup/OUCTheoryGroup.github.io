机器学习/模式识别(Machine Learning and Pattern Recognition)
Feature selection is one of the fundamental problems in the fields of machine learning, pattern recognition and statistics. In my research works, information theory and game theory are applied in feature selection, aiming at finding a good feature subset which produces higher classification accuracy. I also like to apply feature selection algorithms for cancer diagnosis.
图像分析(Image Analysis)
Texture and color are two important cues in numerous processes of human cognition. It is particularly important to object classification. Object recognition and classification are mainly relied on both the texture and color characteristics of the creature surface. However most of the current researches on texture classification are based on gray geometric-based features, which makes many objects misclassified because the missing of color contents. Currently I focus on the research topic of color texture feature extraction and selection.
复杂网络(Complex Networks)
Many things around us can be described as networks: the web of friendships, the internet connection, chains of historical events. This information tells us not only how the network has formed, but more also how dynamical systems (worm speading, information flow, etc) behaves. I devoted myself to find a mathematical model for social network topology evolution and spreading dynamics of social network worms.
社会计算(Social Computing)
Social computing is the research of information technologies and digital media as social and cultural phenomena, such as recommendation systems, interests pre -diction and social network analysis. Computing with spatial trajectories is a fairly young and dynamic research field. I will devote myself to trajectories clustering, classification and prediction.
